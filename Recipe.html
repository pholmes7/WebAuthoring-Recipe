Paul's Pulled Pork Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • Boston Butt
  • Barbecue Sauce
  • Onion
  • Brown Sugar
  • Black Pepper
  • Bread

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees
  2. Chop Onions
  3. Mix Barbecue Sauce, Black Pepper, Brown Sugar, and Onions in a bowl
  4. Shred the Boston Butt and place in the oven for 30 mins
  5. When done apply sauce to shredded Boston Butt
  6. Place meat on the bread
  7. Enjoy!
